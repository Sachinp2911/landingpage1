No two kids are the same, then why should they learn the same thing, the same way and at the same pace as every other kid?

At Mindseed, we believe that every child is unique with innate skills and abilities that need nurturing. With this philosophy at the heart of everything we do we have developed a personlised learning methodology that involves skill-based leassons and engaging activities that enable your child to hone his/her individual abilities.

A learning experience as unique as your child

We customize learning for each and every child based on their strengths, needs and skill level, so that we can keep every child engaged and happy. Here, children learn at their own pace and unlock new levels of learning only once they have mastered an activity.

Where there are immersive learning spaces

Instead of organising the classrooms according to grades or age, we have organised them into different skill stations, as per skill-based curriculum. Each of these skill stations is designed to promote one foundational skill in an immersive atmosphere

Where skill acquisition is more important than rote learning

A child becomes a lifelong learner by acquiring skills, and that's why we emphasize on skill acquisition in the formational years. Our skill-based curriculum ensures that children develop mastery in crucial skills such as reading, writing, numeracy, and socio-emotional skills in their foundational years

Where learning continues at home

personalized learning doesn't stop in the classroom, it continues at home in collaboration with parents through the Mindseed App. Our App makes learning visible to parents and recommends activities to futher your child's learning at home, thus making you a partner in the learning process

Our Programs


    Toddler
    2 yr to 3 yrs
    Kids are exposed to activities that help them become independent, make friends and follow a routine. They are exposed to language through songs and rhymes. Playtime helps kids develop their fine and gross motor skills.
    In play school, children develop the following skills:
  • Self-expression and self-confidence through pretend play and free play.
  • Socio-emotional skills such as independence as they learn to eat on their own and wear their own shoes.
  • Basic language skills through phonics and reading.
  • Pre math skills through the recognition of pattern and shapes
  • Fine and Gross Motor skills through activities like gripping, twisting, squeezing, grasping, stacking, eating with a spoon, pouring water into a glass, building blocks, moulding clay, climbing stairs, balancing on the beam.
  • It serves the purpose of equipping children with the best learning in preschool.

    • Pre-K
    3 yr to 4 yrs
    Kids experience an explosion of language, along with dramatic advancement in fine and gross motor control, problem-solving ability, independence and social interaction. They may begin to read two/three words and can make interesting mathematical patterns.
    In Nursery School, children develop the following skills:
    • Pre math and logic-based skills like identifying patterns, sequencing, size, and quantity
    • Language skills through writing and reading whole words
    • Early reading skills as they are encouraged to become independent readers.

    K1/K2
    4 yr to 6 yrs
    Kids are equipped with the skills to succeed in Grade 1. By the end of KG, children will be able to draw and write their own stories, perform basic maths operations and communicate confidently in front of the class.
    In kindergarten, children develop the following skills:
    • Writing skills through emergent literacy.
    • Advanced language and reading skills through storytelling.
    • Advanced math skills with concepts such as bar graphs and word problems.
    • Public speaking skills through pretend and play and various opportunities such as hosting the class and annual function.

    The Mindseed Way

    The journey started with 3 Vishwajyot K12 schools in 2004 in Maharastra, with a vision to change the world by unlocking each person's learning potential. Today the brand has 25+ preschools across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Thane and Pune and has impacted more than 20,000 kids. Mindseed has ranked #1 company owned preschool and daycare in Mumbai. #1 in innovative teaching in Mumbai and #1 preschool and daycare in India continously for the last 3 years.

    At Mindseed, we believe that every child is genius and we should celebrate them for who they are rather pushing them to conform. That's why at Mindseed, we customize the learning experience for each and every child based on their strengths, needs and skill level to keep them engaged and happy.
